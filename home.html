TAMANG HOMESTAY

Tamang Homestay in Ghoom Bhanjyang, Darjeeling, offers a serene and authentic mountain retreat, blending traditional hospitality with modern comforts. Situated approximately 1 km from Ghoom Railway Station and 9 km from Darjeeling (main town), the homestay provides guests with breathtaking views of the Kanchenjunga range. Visitors consistently commend the establishment for its immaculate cleanliness, warm hospitality, and delectable homestyle meals. The spacious rooms, complete with expansive balconies and a rooftop terrace, allow guests to immerse themselves in the tranquil ambiance of the Himalayas. With a commendable rating of 4.7 out of 5, Tamang Homestay stands out as a top choice for travelers seeking a peaceful getaway amidst nature .

HOW TO REACH TAMANG HOMESTAY

By Car: Drive to Ghoom Bhanjyang from Siliguri via Kurseong (2 hrs) or Mirik (4.5 hrs). Parking available.

By Train: Nearest station is New Jalpaiguri (NJP). Take a cab or shared jeep to Ghoom.

By Air: Fly to Bagdogra Airport. Cabs available to Ghoom Bhanjyang.
